Internet of Things

Internet of Things (IoT) is a modern technology that connects smart devices, sensors, and systems through the internet to automate tasks, collect data, and improve efficiency in daily life and industries.

About IoT Engineering

IoT Engineering is a growing field that combines electronics, networking, programming, and communication technologies to create smart systems.

Students in this field learn about embedded systems, sensors, cloud computing, wireless communication, and automation.

IoT technology is used in smart homes, healthcare systems, industrial automation, transportation, agriculture, and security systems.

Applications of IoT

  • Smart Home Automation
  • Smart Agriculture Systems
  • Healthcare Monitoring
  • Industrial Automation
  • Smart Traffic Management
  • Environmental Monitoring
  • Wearable Devices

Future Scope

The demand for IoT professionals is increasing rapidly due to advancements in artificial intelligence, cloud platforms, and smart devices.

IoT engineers can work in software development, embedded systems, networking, cybersecurity, automation, and data analytics.

As industries continue to adopt smart technologies, IoT will remain one of the important fields in engineering and innovation.
